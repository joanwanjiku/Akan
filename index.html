Akan Naming System

Background

This naming system is practised the by the Akan people in Ghana and a few other west african Countries. Newborns are given special names depending on the day they are born and the order in which they were born. Most west africans have one name from this naming sysytem.

Aside the fact that day names are a simple means for commonality,They have further meanings concerning the soul and character of the person
